About Funky Fiber Works!

Funky Fiber Works is a family owned and operated business which was established in Phoenix, Arizona in 1971. It is the goal of our family business - containing three generations - to provide you with personalized service and top quality merchandise at the most competitive prices available. We encourage you to try a variety of our products. We believe in the true American spirit of free enterprise and will continue providing you with original and unique merchandise that truly represents the atmosphere of the Southwest.

If you are interested in opening a wholesale account with us, please see the Ordering Information section for more details.

P.S.-
All of our invoices, literature, styrofoam, and corrugated cardboard boxes are recyclable. We try to be as conservative as we can when dealing with these items.
