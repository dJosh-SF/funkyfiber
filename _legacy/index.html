Welcome!

Funky Fiber Works is a family-owned crafts business based in Phoenix, Arizona.

Our designs are inspired by the arts of the people of the Native Americas and the American Southwest as well as the look of High Country Lodge.

If you are interested in opening a wholesale account with us, please see the Ordering Information section for more details.
